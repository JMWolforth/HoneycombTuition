Time Travellers History Club

Explore the past as part of a small group of students. Sessions are multi sensory, with audio, video, text and discussion all driving the learning and exploration. All writing is optional, although students will recieve an activity pack to complete if they wish, and again, can have feedback on it if required. Students who attend a block of six sessions will recieve a Certificate, detailing the topics they have studied, on completion of a presentation of what they have learned. This presentation can take any form, and students will be encouraged to be creative. It could be completion of the activity pack, a short story set in the time period they've studied, a craft activity inspired by their work, a drawing of something they've learnt about or an audio recording of their thoughts on a topic, or anything else they can imagine.
