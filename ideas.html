Big Ideas

Life after death? What is thinking? How do we know things? What does existence mean? What are ethics? These are all examples of topics we might cover in our Big Ideas Kid's Philosophy Group. It's mainly a group for discussion, voicing your opinions, learning how to state your point of view, and listening to and respecting the views of others. Groups start their journey throughout the year as spaces are filled. Register your interest to be added to the waiting list for the next group.
