Individual Tuition

I take a slightly different approach to individual tuition, as I specialise in tutoring Home Educated children. Sessions last for 30 minutes, and include homework tasks to be done throughout the week. This means the sessions are focused, quick paced and there is no unnecessary downtime. Individual sessions can be aligned with the National Curriculum (I will assess what level your child is working at), or can focus on specific areas identified in a detailed consultation with you.


What's included?
  • Individually planned lessons by a qualified teacher in any subject or level of your choosing from Early Years to KS3
  • Activity packs related to the lesson's content for students to complete, emailed to you weekly
  • Regular detailed reports of work completed and progress made
  • Optional assessments
